Secure Your Website

What is Website Security?

Web security is a broad category of security solutions that protect networks, computer systems, devices, and data from internet-based cyberattacks, such as malware, phishing, and theft. It involves strategies, infrastructure, training, and monitoring to prevent users from introducing malware and other threats to the network from malicious websites. It also includes firewall inspection, intrusion prevention system scanning, sandboxing, URL filtering, and other security and access controls. Web security is critical to business continuity and to reducing the security risk to organizations

What is Website UX?

User experience (UX) design is the process design teams use to create products that provide meaningful and relevant experiences to users. UX design involves the design of the entire process of acquiring and integrating the product, including aspects of branding, design, usability and function.
